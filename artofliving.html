Art of Living

Sri Sri Ravi Shankar - Meditation App

Art of Living - A meditation application that provides courses and content regarding sleep, stress, enhancing focus, and managing anger. It also offers in-person meetups and workshops where user can go physically and meditate there. It is your guide on the journey towards the self. Whether you are a beginner or have been meditating for years, this app is your best companion to make a happy and stress-free life. Moreover, this app allows users to find and join one of their signature workshops near you. Curated content from our meditation and wellness experts will guide you each step along the way. When you need some stress relief or lessons on creating better sleep conditions, the Art of Living app can help. A meditations lesson from the app will strengthen you and get you ready for your next battle.

Client Requirement

Gurudev established The Art of Living as an international, non-profit, educational, and humanitarian organization. Gurudev Sri Sri Ravi Shankar is a humanitarian leader, spiritual teacher, and an ambassador of peace. His vision of a stress-free, violence-free society has united millions of people worldwide through service projects and programs offered by The Art of Living. Due to rising digital life, a mobile app is the best option to reach millions of people who need stress free experience through meditation, yoga, etc. Art of Living team understood the importance of mobile app and wanted to make a mobile app that will help people to do regular yoga, meditation practice, knowledge nuggets, and more at a single platform. After listening to the app idea and requirements about front-end development and design, our team started working on it and developed & delivered the app within a timeline.

Features

Art of Living - A meditation application features.

Authentication

Users can sign up with necessary detail or sign in with Google or Facebook.

From Instructor

Here, video functionality is integrated where users can get watch videos from the best instructors on meditation.

Expendition

Users can have mini-courses that improve sleep, reduce stress, enhance focus, and managing anger, lead by happiness and wellness experts and meditation instructors. They are a unique blend of practical wisdom, scientific insights as well as breathing and meditation techniques

Meditation

Users can get guidance about meditations by 5 to 10 minutes are designed to ease you into the habit of studying regularly. Bedtime Talks features a collection of lying-down guided meditations designed to make you fall into a deep sleep.

Home

Users can have the following features of the Art of Living App.

Sky journey

Users can learn the SKY breathing technique and embark upon this fun journey. Users need to follow every instruction and unlock a new level every day.

To manage this scenario was a little bit tough as it was new for our developers. After some in-depth research and analysis, we deeply analyze core graphics and create the required UI using some mathematics calculations.
