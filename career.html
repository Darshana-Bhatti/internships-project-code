Process of Interview

Want to be the part of one of us? Here is the interview process we follow to evaluate your performance and welcome you onboard.

1. HR Interview

In the first stage, the candidate will communicate with one of the Human resource representatives who will evaluate the candidate's organizational skills like communication, leadership, decision-making, problem-solving, and more.

2. Personal Interview

In the second stage, the candidate with communicate with the interviewer who will evaluate their technical knowledge and skills based on the job description through a round of questions.

3. Practical Interview

In the last stage, we will evaluate your domain expertise through a practical task where we expect you to provide your best output to be an employee of Hyperlink InfoSystem.
